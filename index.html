Contact Me

I am a second-year Ph.D. student (2023-present) at the Security and Artificial Intelligence Lab (SAIL) and the VinUni-Illinois Smart Health Center (VISHC) , at VinUniversity, supervised by Prof. Kok-Seng Wong and Prof. Khoa D Doan.

My research focuses on Federated Learning, Backdoor Learning, Trustworthy Machine Learning, and Adversarial Machine Learning.

I have developed a Shiny website where I share a curated list of papers, code, and resources related to these research areas.

Previously, I earned my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science from the University of Engineering and Technology.

If you're interested in my research, feel free to reach out to me via email for communication or collaboration.
